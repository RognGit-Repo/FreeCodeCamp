How to cook a scrambled egg

This is a recipe to cook a scrambled egg

Ingredients

  • 2 Egg
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 5 tbps. cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
  2. Cut the onion and tomato
  3. Crack and mix the tomato
  4. Add the onion and tomato in the mixed egg
  5. Add seasoning
  6. Heat the cooking oil
  7. Add some spicies first
  8. Add the egg previously prepared
